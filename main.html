About the WWF

In 1961 the World Wildlife Fund was established to create an international fundraising orgranization to collect funds for conservation groups around the world. Throughout the decades the WWF has worked to purchase thousands of acres of land spread across the world to create conservation lands. For example, in 1973 the WWF purchased 37,000 acres adjacent to Kenya's Lake Nakuru which nearly 30 different bird species are dependent on the lake. The WWF has raised millions of dollars and specific funds to help save the worlds most endagered species. To learn more about the WWF amazing history click here.
